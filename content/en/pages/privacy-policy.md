This Privacy Policy covers personal data processed when you browse our website, use our forms (e.g., trial registration, contact), and interact with our marketing and support channels. - **Content you submit:** messages, trial goals, notes in forms.
- **Usage & technical data:** IP address, device and browser info, pages viewed, timestamps, referral source (via analytics and server logs).
- **Account/contract data (customers):** billing/contact persons, subscription status, invoicing details.

## 4. Sources of Data

## 9. Data Retention

We keep personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described above. Specifically:

- **Trial registrations & contact form data:** retained for the duration of the trial and up to **24 months after the last interaction**, unless you request earlier deletion.
- **Customer contract & billing data:** retained for the contract term and thereafter for as long as required by law (typically **up to 5 years** under Polish accounting/tax rules).
- **Analytics data (Google Analytics):** retained according to our analytics settings (currently **up to 14 months**).
- **Server & security logs:** typically **up to 180 days** for security and fraud-prevention.

After these periods, we delete or irreversibly anonymize the data.
